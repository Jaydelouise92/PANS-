## 2025-05-15 - [Skip to Main Content]
**Learning:** For optimal keyboard navigation, a 'Skip to main content' link must be the first focusable element. The target must include `tabIndex={-1}` and `outline-none` to receive programmatic focus cleanly.
**Action:** Implement 'Skip to main content' in `App.tsx` and ensure it's visually hidden until focused.
